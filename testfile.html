How to Share

 

Follow these steps to share your subscription.
 
1. Start at myaccount.al.com.
 
 

2. If already signed in, Sign Out before proceeding.
 
 

3. Click the big 'ACTIVATE' button.
 
 

4. Click the big 'CONTINUE' button.
 
 

6. Set up a password for each account then click 'Create Account'.
 
 

8. Select a look up option from the pulldown AND enter it below.
 
 

9. Click 'Link Subscription.'

 

10. Don't forget to share this information with that family member.
